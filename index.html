About Me

I am an AI and IT Project Manager with a strong focus on leveraging technology to address complex challenges in health, education, and social impact. My work involves leading cross-functional teams and conducting research-driven projects that combine cutting-edge technology with user-centered design. I specialize in integrating AI-powered solutions, digital platforms, and open-source tools to create scalable and sustainable impact.

In addition to project management, I have extensive experience working with governments, NGOs, and global organizations to deliver innovative solutions in low- and middle-income countries (LMICs). My goal is to bridge technology and human-centered design to ensure that digital solutions create measurable impact where it matters most.
